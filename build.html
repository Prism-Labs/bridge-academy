The Bridge Build Challenge

Starting Monday April 8th through April 30th.

Each month new applicants to join Bridge.Academy participate in a build challenge in order to demonstrate their ability to quickly pick up new skills through working on a project of your choice.

Applicants receive points based on their progress. The more impressive your pace of improvement, the higher your score will be and better your chances for being accepted to our program.

How it works

Apply with any project which leverages the skills + technologies that you'd like to learn and improve on.

Apply with anything

First signup for an account and fill our our application form(s). Past applicants have built niche Social Networks, Decentralized Contact Managers, AI News Readers + more. You can read more about some of the past projects here.

Share your progress

Every week you'll receive feedback and points based on your progress. Each month, the top-ranked applicants get accepted to join Bridge and scholarships are awarded based on merit.

Learn from our mentors

Bridge has a diverse network of mentors across different disciplines. Mentors both review applications and provide advice to Bridge members. After 3 months you'll present your project.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bridge a Coding Bootcamp?

No. Our program is built for experienced developers, looking to gain new skills and uplevel their careers.

How long does the core program last?

The build challenge lasts for 4 weeks. The part-time core program lasts for twelve weeks. The full-time program lasts for sixteen weeks.

Is playing in the build challenge required to join Bridge?

Participating in the build challenge is not required, but it greatly increases your chances of being accepted and earning a scholarship.

Do I need a project idea in order to join?

You don't need your own project in order to join, you can select from our list. Your project should reflect the skills you would like to pick up and enhance when joining Bridge and related to the career track you would like to join.

Can I participate from anywhere?

As long as you have a reliable internet connection you can join the build challenge.

Can I participate while employed?

Sure! Your application will be judged based on your progress you make, not how many hours you put in.
